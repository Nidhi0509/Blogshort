Barcelona look to end a successful 2018/19 La Liga season as they travel to Eibar

Alankrit Mathur     May 18,2018       categorized

Barcelona will play Eibar on Sunday in 2018/19 La Liga to finish the season. Barca go into this game as the favourites.
Kick-off : 4:15 PM (Local)
Venue : Municipal de Ipurua, Eibar
Barcelona after a pretty successful domestic season in which they won the La Liga will eye a good end. They were fantastic and played some brilliant football. Now focus should shift on the Copa del Rey.
As of now Barca are first in the points table after 37 matches with 86 points. Against Eibar away they will look to play their brand of football for the three points.
Barcelona will play next Valencia in the Copa del Rey on the 25th May.
On the other hand, Eibar had an OK season and will look to finish the season on a high.
Eibar are currently twelfth in the points table after 37 games with 46 points. In Barcelona they have got tough opponents to end the season but home advantage should come into play and motivate to play their best football.
Barcelona have had a good record against Eibar and they will look to continue the good work.
Team news and possible line-ups
Anaitz Arbilla and Ruben Pena will both be not available for Eibar for the Barcelona game as due to injuries. While Pedro Bigas, Marc Cardona and Paulo Oliveira are all doubtful.
As for Barca, Philippe Coutinho, Ousmane Dembele, Luis Suarez and Rafinha will all be unavailable as due to injuries for Eibar match. Also Arthur is a doubt.
Eibar XI : Dmitrovic, De Blasis, S. Alvarez, I. Ramis, Cote, J. Jordan, Diop, P. Leon, Orellana, Charles, S. Enrich.
Barcelona XI : Ter Stegen, N. Semedo, Pique, Lenglet, J. Alba, Vidal, Busquets, Rakitic, Malcom, Messi, A. Ruiz.
Prediction
Barca should come out on top to bring a fabulous end to a winning season. Them to win 2-0.
Futbol Club Barcelona (Catalan pronunciation: [fubˈbɔl ˈklub bəɾsəˈlonə] (About this soundlisten)), commonly referred to as Barcelona and colloquially known as Barça ([ˈbaɾsə]), is a Spanish professional football club based in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Founded in 1899 by a group of Swiss, Spanish, English, and Catalan footballers led by Joan Gamper, the club has become a symbol of Catalan culture and Catalanism, hence the motto "Més que un club" ("More than a club"). Unlike many other football clubs, the supporters own and operate Barcelona. It is the fourth-most valuable sports team in the world, worth $4.06 billion, and the world's richest football club in terms of revenue, with an annual turnover of €840,8 million.[2][3] The official Barcelona anthem is the "Cant del Barça", written by Jaume Picas and Josep Maria Espinàs.
